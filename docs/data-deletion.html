Account & Data Deletion Request

Request the deletion of your account and associated data from Sagacious Apps

About Data Deletion

At Sagacious Apps, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request either full account deletion or partial data deletion depending on your needs.

Option 1: Full Account Deletion

When you request full account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Google account credentials, display name, email address, profile photo
  • User-Generated Content: All saved and favorited stories you've created
  • Purchase History: All in-app purchase records, unlocked story collections, and remaining credits
  • Usage Data: App usage analytics and interaction history
  • Authentication Data: User ID and last sign-in timestamps

Important: Full account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to any purchased content, unlocked story collections, and remaining credits. You will need to create a new account to use the app again.

Option 2: Partial Data Deletion (Keep Account Active)

If you want to clear your data but keep your account for future use, we can delete the following:

  • User-Generated Content: All saved and favorited stories you've created
  • Story Timestamps: Creation and modification dates for your stories
  • Cloud Sync Data: Backup copies of your stories stored in our cloud database
  • Usage Data: App interaction history and analytics

Data that will be retained with partial deletion:

  • Account Information: Google authentication data (name, email, profile photo)
  • Purchase History: Your in-app purchases, unlocked collections, and remaining credits
  • Last Sign-In: Your most recent login timestamp

With partial deletion, your account remains active and you can continue using the app. Your purchased content and unlocked story collections will be preserved, but all stories you've created will be permanently deleted.

Timeline

Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted from our systems.
